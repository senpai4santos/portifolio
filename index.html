A bit about me

I'm a junior developer based in Salvaterra, focused on turning ideas into clean, working software. My journey started with static sites for local restaurants and grew into full-stack web apps and mobile solutions for the Câmara Municipal de Salvaterra and a school-institution platform.

I love the moment a feature goes live and a real person uses it — whether that's a landing page that converts visitors, or a Flutter app that helps a public service run smoother.

Right now I'm deepening my skills in React, Node.js and Flutter, while shipping real client work on the side. Looking for a junior role where I can learn fast, contribute honestly, and grow with a strong team.
